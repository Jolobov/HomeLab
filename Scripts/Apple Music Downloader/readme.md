## Prerequisites

### Required

- **Python 3.10 or higher**
- **Gamdl ([Glomatico's Apple Music Downloader](https://github.com/glomatico/gamdl))**
- **Apple Music Cookies** - Export your browser cookies in Netscape format by logging in with an active subscription to the Apple Music website.
  - **Firefox** - [Export Cookies](https://addons.mozilla.org/addon/export-cookies-txt)
  - **Chromium** - [Get cookies.txt LOCALLY](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/get-cookiestxt-locally/cclelndahbckbenkjhflpdbgdldlbecc)
